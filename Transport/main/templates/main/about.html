About Wesal

Scheduled Transportation Platform

Wesal is a Saudi technology-driven transportation platform aligned with the objectives of Saudi Vision 2030. It delivers an innovative solution to daily commuting challenges through a scheduled, subscription-based transportation system built on reliability, planning, and consistency.

Wesal was created to serve individuals with recurring transportation needs—such as employees, students, and families—who depend on stable and dependable mobility that ensures punctuality, safety, and peace of mind, without the uncertainty of on-demand ride services.

Planned & Scheduled Mobility

Wesal operates through predefined routes and fixed daily schedules, enabling users to plan their transportation in advance and rely on consistent service without repeated daily bookings.

Reliable & Secure Experience

A subscription-based model, clear trip details, and consistent drivers ensure a safe, transparent, and trustworthy commuting experience for all users.

Community-Focused Transportation

Wesal promotes organized shared transportation that reduces daily stress, optimizes resources, and contributes to improved quality of life within the community.

Our Vision

To redefine daily transportation in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia by transitioning from unpredictable, request-based services to a smart, structured, and sustainable mobility system that enhances quality of life and supports the nation’s digital transformation under Vision 2030.

Our Mission

To provide a dependable, subscription-based transportation solution that ensures punctuality, safety, and ease of planning for users with long-term commuting needs, while maintaining operational efficiency and service stability.

Our Core Values

  • Trust, transparency, and clarity in every journey
  • Commitment to safety, reliability, and consistency
  • Simplicity and flexibility in user experience
  • Supporting organized and sustainable mobility solutions
Proudly built in Saudi Arabia to support Saudi Vision 2030 and enable smarter, more organized daily transportation
